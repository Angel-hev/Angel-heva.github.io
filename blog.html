What I Love

I’ve always loved the adrenaline of playing volleyball, the same rush I feel when riding my motorcycle on open roads—especially during a breathtaking sunset that paints the sky with colors of peace.

On quieter days, you’ll find me focused on a game of billiards, something that started thanks to my brother. I also enjoy joining pageants—whether in casual matches or local competitions—where I embrace both grace and passion.

But nothing beats those warm moments with my family, sharing laughter and stories over a comforting cup of coffee. I really love coffee, especially when I feel sad or overwhelmed.

I cherish every adventure with my boyfriend, Dale—whether we're exploring new places or just being silly together. And of course, I always look forward to spontaneous hangouts with my bestfriend, Karlyn, whose energy never fails to brighten my day.

Life feels complete when it’s a perfect mix of sports, simple joys, and being with the people I love—even if it’s just another night playing billiards and making memories.
